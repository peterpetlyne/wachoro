Student Life at Wachoro Boys

At Wachoro Boys High School, we believe in holistic education that balances academic excellence with character development, extracurricular activities, and community engagement. Our students enjoy a vibrant campus life that prepares them for success beyond the classroom.

Assembly Ground Photo

Morning Assembly

Each day begins with a morning assembly where students gather for prayers, announcements, and motivational talks. This time fosters unity, discipline, and sets the tone for the day's activities.

Prize Giving Day Photo

Prize Giving Day

Our annual prize giving day celebrates academic excellence, talent, and character. Students are recognized for their achievements in academics, sports, leadership, and community service.

Dining Hall Photo

Dining Experience

Our spacious dining hall provides nutritious meals in a clean, organized environment. Meal times are not just about eating but also about building community and practicing etiquette.
