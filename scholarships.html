Scholarships and Bursaries

At Wachoro Boys High School, we believe that financial constraints should not hinder academic excellence. We offer various scholarships and bursaries to support our students in achieving their educational goals.

KenGen Foundation Scholarships

Top performing students in KCSE examinations are sponsored by the KenGen Foundation for their university education.

KenGen Foundation Logo

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a current student at Wachoro Boys High School
  • Must achieve an A grade in KCSE examinations
  • Must demonstrate leadership qualities and good character
  • Must come from a financially disadvantaged background
  • Must have participated in co-curricular activities

Benefits

Full tuition coverage

Book allowance

Accommodation support

Laptop provision

Application Process

  1. Submit application form available from the principal's office
  2. Provide KCSE results slip
  3. Submit recommendation letters from teachers
  4. Write a personal statement
  5. Attend an interview with the selection committee

Government Bursaries

Form 1, 2, 3, and 4 students receive bursaries from the government to support their secondary education.

Government of Kenya Logo

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a Kenyan citizen
  • Must be enrolled at Wachoro Boys High School
  • Must come from a financially disadvantaged background
  • Must maintain good academic performance
  • Must have good discipline records

Benefits

Partial fee payment

Boarding support

Uniform allowance

Transport facilitation

Application Process

  1. Collect application forms from class teachers
  2. Submit completed forms with required documents
  3. Provide proof of financial need
  4. Wait for vetting by the bursary committee
  5. Receive award notification letter

Eligible Students

KenGen Foundation Scholarship Recipients

  • Form 4 students who excel in KCSE examinations
  • Students pursuing STEM courses at university
  • Students with demonstrated leadership capabilities

Government Bursary Recipients

  • Form 1, 2, 3, and 4 students from needy backgrounds
  • Orphans and vulnerable children
  • Students from marginalized communities
  • Students with special needs

Our Commitment

At Wachoro Boys High School, we are committed to ensuring that no student misses out on quality education due to financial constraints. Our scholarship and bursary programs are designed to support deserving students in achieving their academic dreams.
