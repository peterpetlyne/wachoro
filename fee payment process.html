Important Notice Regarding Payment Methods

Please note that NO CASH PAYMENTS are accepted at the school accounts office. All payments must be made through bank transfer or mobile money as described above. This policy ensures the safety and security of all transactions and provides a clear audit trail for both parents and the school administration.

Fee Payment Process Timeline

  1. Receive Fee Structure

    Fee structures are sent to parents at the beginning of each term via email and SMS.

  2. Make Payment

    Use either bank transfer or mobile money payment methods described above.

  3. Send Proof of Payment

    Email a copy of the transaction receipt to accounts@wachoroboys.sc.ke

  4. Payment Verification

    Our accounts office will verify your payment within 24-48 hours.

  5. Receive Confirmation

    You will receive an SMS confirmation once payment is verified.

  6. Fee Statement Update

    Your child's fee statement will be updated within 72 hours of payment verification.

Our Commitment

At Wachoro Boys High School, we are committed to providing a transparent, secure, and efficient fee payment process. Our accounts office is available to assist with any payment-related inquiries and ensure a smooth experience for all parents and guardians.
