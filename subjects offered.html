Comprehensive Curriculum for Holistic Education

At Wochoro Boys High School, we offer a wide range of subjects designed to provide our students with a well-rounded education and prepare them for success in university and beyond.

Mathematics

Develop logical thinking and problem-solving skills through our comprehensive mathematics program.

Chemistry

Explore the composition, structure, and properties of matter through hands-on laboratory experiments.

Physics

Understand the fundamental principles governing the universe, from subatomic particles to galaxies.

Biology

Study living organisms and their interactions with each other and the environment.

Geography

Explore Earth's landscapes, environments, and the relationships between people and their environments.

English Language

Master communication skills and appreciate literature from around the world.

History

Understand past events, their causes and effects, and how they shape our present and future.

Business Studies

Learn the fundamentals of business operations, management, and entrepreneurship.

Computer Studies

Develop digital literacy and programming skills essential for the modern world.

Our Educational Approach

At Wochoro Boys High School, we believe in providing a balanced education that combines academic excellence with character development. Our curriculum is designed to nurture critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills that prepare students for the challenges of the 21st century.
