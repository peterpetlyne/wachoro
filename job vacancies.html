Employment Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated educators and staff at Wachoro Boys High School. We are committed to providing quality education and are looking for passionate professionals to help us achieve our mission.

Mathematics Teacher

We are seeking a qualified and enthusiastic Mathematics Teacher to join our dedicated faculty. The ideal candidate will have a passion for teaching and the ability to inspire students to excel in mathematics.

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Education with a specialization in Mathematics
  • Registered with the Teachers Service Commission (TSC)
  • Minimum of 3 years teaching experience in a secondary school
  • Strong knowledge of the Kenyan secondary education curriculum
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Ability to use technology to enhance teaching and learning

Responsibilities

  • Prepare lesson plans and teach mathematics to students
  • Develop and implement engaging instructional strategies
  • Assess and evaluate student progress regularly
  • Participate in curriculum development programs
  • Provide guidance and counseling to students
  • Participate in co-curricular activities

Physics Teacher

We are looking for a passionate Physics Teacher to join our science department. The ideal candidate will have a strong background in physics and the ability to make complex concepts understandable and exciting for students.

Qualifications & Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Education with a specialization in Physics
  • Registered with the Teachers Service Commission (TSC)
  • Minimum of 3 years teaching experience in a secondary school
  • Thorough knowledge of physics concepts and principles
  • Experience in laboratory management and safety protocols
  • Ability to inspire students to pursue STEM careers

Responsibilities

  • Teach physics to secondary school students
  • Prepare and conduct physics practical experiments
  • Develop instructional materials and assessment tools
  • Maintain physics laboratory equipment and ensure safety
  • Guide students in science projects and competitions
  • Participate in departmental meetings and school events

Benefits of Working at Wachoro Boys High School

  • Competitive salary package commensurate with experience
  • Medical insurance coverage
  • Housing allowance or accommodation provided
  • Professional development opportunities
  • Supportive work environment
  • Opportunities for career advancement
  • Paid school holidays

Application Process

  1. Prepare Application Documents

    Prepare your application letter, CV, copies of academic and professional certificates, and TSC registration certificate.

  2. Submit Application

    Email your application to the address provided below. Use the position you are applying for as the subject of your email.

  3. Application Review

    Our recruitment team will review all applications and contact shortlisted candidates within two weeks of application deadline.

  4. Interviews

    Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview and possibly a demonstration lesson.

  5. Offer

    Successful candidates will receive an offer letter and details about the next steps.
