Directions to the School

Follow these directions to easily find Wachoro Boys High School:

1

From Nairobi

Take the Nairobi-Nyeri Highway heading north. Continue past Thika and Makuyu. The school is located approximately 100km from Nairobi, just after Sagana Bridge.

2

From Nyeri

Take the Nyeri-Nairobi Highway heading south. Pass through Karatina and continue towards Sagana. The school is located about 2km after Sagana town.

3

From Embu/Meru

Take the Embu-Nairobi road until you reach Makutano junction. Turn left onto the Nairobi-Nyeri Highway. The school is located 700m from Makutano Market.

4

Using Public Transport

Take a matatu heading to Nyeri from Nairobi or vice versa. Ask to be dropped at Wachoro Boys High School junction. The school is a short walk from the main road.
