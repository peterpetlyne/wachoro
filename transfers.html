Student Transfer Policy

At Wachoro Boys High School, we have established clear guidelines for student transfers to ensure a smooth transition for both incoming and outgoing students while maintaining our academic standards.

Transfer Into the School

We welcome students who meet our academic standards and share our commitment to excellence. The following requirements must be met for admission:

Academic Requirements

  • Minimum mean grade of C+ and above from the previous school
  • Good conduct record from the previous school

Documentation Requirements

  • Clearance letter from the previous school showing reasons for transfer
  • Academic transcripts from the previous school
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical records

Financial Requirements

  • Full payment of fees for the current term
  • Payment of all applicable admission fees

Transfer Out of the School

For students leaving Wachoro Boys High School, the following procedures must be followed:

Financial Clearance

  • Clear all outstanding school balances
  • Settle any pending fee obligations
  • Return all school property in good condition

Departmental Clearance

  • Clear with the Library department
  • Clear with the Laboratory department
  • Clear with the Games department
  • Clear with the Dormitory department
  • Clear with the Academic department

Administrative Process

  • Complete the transfer form
  • Obtain necessary signatures from all departments
  • Receive final clearance from the Principal's office

Important Notice Regarding Transfer Intake

Please note that no transfer intake is allowed for Form Twos and Threes. Transfers are only considered for Form One and Form Four students, and even then, only under exceptional circumstances with approval from the Board of Management.

Transfer Process Timeline

  1. Application Submission

    Submit all required documents and application forms at least 30 days before the beginning of the term.

  2. Document Review

    Our admissions committee will review your application within 10 working days.

  3. Entrance Assessment

    Shortlisted candidates may be required to sit for an entrance assessment.

  4. Interview

    Selected candidates and parents/guardians will be invited for an interview.

  5. Admission Decision

    Successful applicants will receive an admission letter within 5 working days after the interview.

  6. Fee Payment

    Fees must be paid in full before the student reports to school.

Our Commitment

At Wachoro Boys High School, we are committed to providing a seamless transfer process for all students. Our administration is available to guide you through every step of the process and answer any questions you may have.
