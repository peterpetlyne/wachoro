WACHORO BOYS HIGH SCHOOL HISTORY

Founded in 1925, Wachoro Boys High School has grown from a small local Mixed Day school to a renowned institution of excellence in education.

Blessed Joseph Allamano

Founder

Original School Building (1985)

The school started with just 35 students and 3 teachers

Current Campus (2025)

Modern facilities while preserving our historic values

1985

Blessed Joseph Allamano co-workers establishes Wachoro Mixed Day School with a vision to provide quality education to the community. The school begins with 35 students in a small building.

1995

After the community witnesed the good grades from the school, they brought more students to school there .

2003

The schoolgains popularity and political leaders decide to support the school. New science labs and a library are added.

2010

Wachoro Mixed Day School becomes one of the first schools in the region to introduce computer education with the acquisition of 10 personal computers.

2012

The school population increases and it had to be made a boy school. Girls were build another school called Gitaraka Girls School.

2020

The school becomes a national school with over 20% national students and 2% international students.

2025

Wachoro School is recognized as a National School of Excellence, with over 1,200 students and 85 faculty members.

Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. Our mission has always been to ignite curiosity and passion for learning in every student.
