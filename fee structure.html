Important Notes

1. All fees are payable in full at the beginning of each term.

2. A discount of 5% is offered for full payment before the term begins.

3. Payment plans are available for parents who need flexibility. Please contact the accounts office.

4. Late payment will attract a penalty of 2% per month on the outstanding balance.

5. The school accepts payments through bank transfer or mobile money. No cash payments are allowed.

Financial Assistance

Wachoro Boys High School offers scholarships and bursaries to deserving students based on both academic performance and financial need. Please contact the principal's office for more information about financial assistance programs.
