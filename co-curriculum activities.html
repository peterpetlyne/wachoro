Our Philosophy on Co-Curricular Activities

At Wachoro Boys High School, we believe that co-curricular activities are essential for developing well-rounded individuals. These activities complement academic learning by fostering teamwork, leadership, discipline, and creativity. Our program is designed to help students discover and nurture their talents while building character and resilience.
