Tender Opportunities

Wachoro Boys High School invites qualified and interested suppliers to apply for our available tender opportunities. We are committed to transparency, fairness, and value for money in all our procurement processes.

Food Supply Tender

Wachoro Boys High School is seeking qualified suppliers to provide food supplies for the academic year 2024. The successful bidder will be responsible for supplying quality food items to our school kitchen.

Tender Number

WBHS/F/001/2023

Closing Date

15th December, 2023 at 10:00 AM

Tender Opening

15th December, 2023 at 11:00 AM

Contract Period

1st January, 2024 - 31st December, 2024

Items Required

  • Fresh vegetables and fruits
  • Cereals (maize, rice, wheat)
  • Dairy products (milk, yogurt)
  • Meat and poultry products
  • Cooking oil
  • Bread and bakery products
  • Other dry food supplies

Requirements for Applicants

  • Valid business registration certificate
  • Valid tax compliance certificate
  • Proof of experience in similar supplies
  • Certificate of incorporation (for companies)
  • KRA PIN certificate
  • Quality assurance certification
  • Recommendation letters from previous clients

Application Process

  1. Obtain Tender Documents

    Download all tender documents from our website or collect them from the school office.

  2. Prepare Your Bid

    Complete all required documents and provide all necessary supporting materials.

  3. Submit Your Bid

    Submit your sealed bid in the tender box at the school office before the closing date and time.

  4. Tender Opening

    Attend the tender opening session at the specified date and time.

  5. Evaluation

    Our tender committee will evaluate all bids based on specified criteria.

  6. Award Notification

    Successful bidders will be notified within 14 days after tender opening.

Our Procurement Principles

At Wachoro Boys High School, we adhere to the principles of transparency, fairness, competition, and value for money in all our procurement processes. We are committed to ethical business practices and equal opportunity for all qualified suppliers.
