Admission Requirements

To be considered for admission, applicants must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • Completed junior secondary education with a recognized junior secondary school certificate
  • Minimum grade of C+ and above
  • Official certificate from all previously attended institutions
  • Proof of English language proficiency (for non-native speakers)
  • Completed application form with all required documents
  • Application fee payment receipt

Admission Period

Admissions are offered every year from January 6 to January 15.

Applications submitted outside this period will not be considered for the current academic year.

Rules for Admission

Please carefully review the following rules and regulations regarding the admission process:

  1. All applications must be submitted through the official online portal. Paper applications will not be accepted.
  2. Submitted documents become the property of the school and will not be returned.
  3. Providing false information or forged documents will result in immediate disqualification and may lead to legal action.
  4. Admission decisions are final and cannot be appealed except in cases of procedural error.
  5. Accepted students must confirm their enrollment by the specified deadline and pay the required enrollment fee.
  6. The school reserves the right to revoke admission if final grades do not meet the minimum requirements.
  7. Deferral of admission may be granted under exceptional circumstances, subject to approval by the admissions committee.
  8. All admitted students must undergo a mandatory orientation program before the beginning of classes.
  9. The school maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards any form of discrimination in the admission process.

Important: These rules are subject to change. Please check our website regularly for updates.
